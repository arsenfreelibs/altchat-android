altchat.me — User Policies

This page contains the main policies and agreements governing the use of the altchat.me messaging platform.

1. User Agreement & Privacy Policy

By using altchat.me, users agree to comply with these terms.

User obligations

  • Use the service only for lawful purposes
  • Do not distribute illegal or harmful content
  • Do not engage in fraud, spam, or malware distribution
  • Respect rights of other users and third parties

Data processing

The service may process limited technical information necessary for operation including phone number, username, device information, IP address, and connection logs.

Data is used for service functionality, security, fraud prevention, and technical support.

Law enforcement

User data may be disclosed to authorized authorities only when legally required.

2. Content Moderation

Users may report content that:

  • violates applicable law
  • contains fraud or scams
  • spreads malware
  • violates intellectual property rights
  • contains threats or harassment

Moderation actions may include warning, restriction, content removal, or permanent account suspension.

3. App Compliance

altchat.me is a communication platform designed for lawful messaging between users.

  • text messaging
  • file sharing
  • voice communication
  • group chats

The service is provided "as is" without guarantee of uninterrupted operation.

4. Law Enforcement Requests

Requests for user data are reviewed only if submitted by authorized authorities and compliant with applicable law.

Official requests: legal@altchat.me

5. Anti‑Spam Policy

  • bulk unsolicited messaging is prohibited
  • phishing and scam links are prohibited
  • mass advertising without consent is prohibited

Accounts violating spam rules may be restricted or permanently banned.

Spam reports: support@altchat.me
